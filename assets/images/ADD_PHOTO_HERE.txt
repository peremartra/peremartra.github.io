ADD YOUR PHOTO HERE
===================

Save your profile photo as: profile.jpg

Requirements:
- Format: JPG or PNG
- File name: profile.jpg (exactly, lowercase)
- Recommended size: 400x400px minimum
- Type: Professional photo, clean background

The photo will display as a 160px circle on the page.

If you don't have a photo ready:
1. Use your LinkedIn photo
2. Take a quick photo with neutral background
3. Use a professional headshot if you have one

To add your photo:
1. Delete this ADD_PHOTO_HERE.txt file
2. Add your profile.jpg in this folder (assets/images/)
